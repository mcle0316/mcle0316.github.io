About Me

I am a young fresh graphic designer, about to enter the real world. Currently finishing up my last year of graphic design at Algonquin College.

I am an ambisious and open minded graphic designer who lives in ottawa. I am currently a student at Algonquin college, finishing up my last year of graphic design. As a part time job/hobby I coach recreational and preschool gymnastics. When I'm not doing school or work I like to spend time outside, taking my dog to the park or going atving with the family.

Why do I design? I design because I am a visual person. I think in pictures, colours, and shapes more than I can think in words. It is the way I express myself. I find it very hard to form full thoughts from my head into sentences on a piece of paper. However when it comes to design it comes more naturally. Designing is my passion. It is something that I like to do all the time. Theres always a new challenge, a problem to solve. And although I am terrible at math problems, I am good at solving design problems.
